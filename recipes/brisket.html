Brisket

Description

A BIG smokey piece of beef!

Ingredients

  • Full packer brisket
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season with salt & pepper
  2. Heat smoker to 225 degrees
  3. Cook until brikset is at 160 degrees
  4. Wrap in foil
  5. Continue cooking until meat reaches 203 degrees
  6. Remove from smoker and allow to sit in Cambro for 2 hours
