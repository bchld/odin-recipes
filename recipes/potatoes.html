Potatoes

Description

Quartered crispy potatoes!

Ingredients

  • 8 petite red potatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Season with salt & pepper
  2. Heat Big Easy to full temp
  3. Place in potatoes in top basket
  4. Cook for 40 min
  5. Place crisper lid on Big East
  6. Cook for an additional 8 min
